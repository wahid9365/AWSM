Main properties of Multimedia system

  1. Combination of media:

    Every arbitrary combination of media cannot be justified as multimedia. A text processing program with incorporated image is not a multimedia application. Multimedia system incorporates both continuous and discrete media.

  2. Independence:

    An important aspect of different media is their level of independence from each other. A computer controlled video recorder stores audio and video information, but there is a inherently tight connection between two types of media. Both media are coupled together through the common storage medium.

  3. Computer supported Integration

    The media independence pre-requisite provides the possibility of combining media in arbitrary forms. Computers are the ideal tool for this purpose. Computer- controlled data of independent media can be integrated to accomplish certain functions.
