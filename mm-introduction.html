Introduction

Multimedia has become an inevitable part of any presentation. It has found a variety of applications right from entertainment to education. The evolution of internet has also increased the demand for multimedia content.


Multimedia : Multimedia is the field concerned with the computer-controlled integration of text, graphics, drawings, still and moving images (Video), animation, audio, and any other media where every type of information can be represented, stored, transmitted and processed digitally.

Multimedia composed of two word:

Multi- means many; multiple

Medium: an intervening substances through which something is transmitted or carried on; A means of mass communication such as newspaper, magazine or television.

A multimedia system is characterized by computer controlled, integrated production, manipulation, presentation, storage and communication of independent information which is encoded at least through a continuous ( time-dependent) and discrete ( time-independent) medium.

  • The integration of the different media into the computer provides additional possibilities for the use of computational power currently available. For example, interactive presentation of huge amount of information.
  • In multimedia system, data can be transmitted through computer and telecommunication networks, which implies in application in the areas of information distribution and cooperative work.
  • One of the first and best known institutes working on different aspects of multimedia is the MIT Media Lab in Boston. Research in going on there on a variety of future applications such as personal newspaper and holography.

Medium

Medium can be describes as a means for distribution and presentation of information. Examples of a medium are text, graphics, speech and music.
