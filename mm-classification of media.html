Media can be classified with respect to different criteria. Classification of media according to perception, representation, presentation, storage, transmission and information exchange.

The perception medium

  • The perception media help humans to sense their environment. The perception of information occurs mostly through seeing or hearing the information.
  • For the perception of information through seeing, the visual media sush as text, image and video are used.
  • For perception of information through hearing, auditory media such as music, noise and speech are relevant.

The Representation Medium

Representation media are characterized by internal computer representation of information. The various formats are used to represent media information in a computer. For example,

  • A text character is coded in ASCII or EBCDIC code.
  • Graphic are coded according to CEPT or CAPTAIN videotext standard
  • An image can be coded in JPEG format
  • A combined audio/video sequence can be coded in different TV standard formats (eg. PAL) and stored in the computer using an MPEG format.

The presentation medium

The presentation medium refers to the tools and devices for the input and output of information. The media such as paper, screen and speaker are used to deliver the information by the computer (output media); keyboard, mouse, camera and microphone are the input media.

The storage media

Storage media refer to a data carrier which enables storage of information. Hard disk, CD/DVD are examples of storage media.

The transmission media

The transmission media refers to different information carier, that enables continuous data transmission. The information is transmitted over networks, which are wire and cable transmission such as coaxial cable as well as wireless transmission.

The information exchange medium

The information exchange medium includes all information carriers for transmission i.e. all storage and transmission media.

Representation Space and Representation values

  • Representation space can be considered as a part of the presentation media for information output. Examples of visual representation spaces are paper or screen. During a computer-controlled slide show with simultaneous projection of the computer screen content, the whole screen count as a representation spaces.
  • Representation values determine the information representation of different of media. For example, text medium visually represents a sequence through a sequence of characters.
