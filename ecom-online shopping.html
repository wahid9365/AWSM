Online Shopping:

Online shopping is a form of electronic commerce which allows consumers to directly buy goods or services from a seller over the Internet using a web browser or a mobile app.

Advantages of Online Shopping :

  • Aves time and efforts.
  • The convenience of shopping at home.
  • Wide variety/range of products are available.
  • Good discounts / lower prices.
  • Get detailed information about the product.
  • We can compare various models/brands.

Disadvantages of online shopping :

  • Delay in delivery
  • Lack of significant discounts in online shops
  • Missing the shopping experience
  • Frauds in online shopping

What is a brief description of Amazon company?

Amazon.com Inc (Amazon) is an online retailer and web service provider. The company provides products such as apparel, auto and industrial items, beauty and health products, electronics, grocery, games, jewelry, kids and baby products, music, sports goods, toys, and tools.

What is a brief description of Flipkart company?

Flipkart Private Limited is an Indian e-commerce company established in 2007. It started with a primary focus on online book sales and soon, expanded to lifestyle products, electronics, home essentials and groceries. Today, Flipkart is the biggest online Indian marketplace competing with the world leader Amazon.

What is best about Snapdeal?

Snapdeal has emerged as a popular online shopping website, offering a diverse range of products, competitive prices, and satisfactory customer service. As a reliable platform, it provides a convenient and hassle-free shopping experience to its users.
